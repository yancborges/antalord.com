About

Since i was a child i love pokemon, and that passion grew when i discovered the nintendo games. And i play then until today!
But, playing the same game for years can be annoying, so taking challenges can make it be fun again.
Sometime ago, i took the Japanese version of the game for studying, and so, i decided to play it with challenges for being more fun. This app can help you to play with a surprise pokemon party, and having fun searching for them!

Notes
  • Type option is off for now
  • For default, is not possible to have a copy of pokemon in the party (at least for now)
  • For now, only final stages of pokemon (sorry, no full ratata party)
  • For default, pokemons only able to get with by trading are not on the list
  • Also, only 6 member parties too
  • Option of legendaries in the party will be added later, for now they are in (s2 articuno)
"Why?" you'd ask. Well, i have to write all data about the pokemons manually. And i'm lazy. So no, no way i'll do that.
